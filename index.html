About Career Mind


Hi, I am Tali Litvak-Kor, owner of Career Mind Consulting company.

I love working with people and always look for new and efficient ways to meet my client’s goals. Being an energetic, dedicated and motivated individual with fluency in English, Russian and Hebrew, I bring international recruitment skills, diverse knowledge and a real passion to my work.

With 7 years of hands-on experience in Human Resources, Recruitment and Talent Sourcing for a variety of industries, I have learned to distinguish between what Candidate wants and what Hiring Manager needs.

I am offering separate services for Job Seekers and Hiring Managers. Please head to Services section for a complete list of the provided services.
